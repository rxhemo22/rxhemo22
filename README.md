# 💫 About Me:
💻 I build, break, and rebuild things until they work better than before.  
I enjoy the process of turning ideas into real, working solutions—and then refining them until they’re clean, efficient, and scalable. For me, it’s not enough for code to just “work”; it should be readable, maintainable, and built with purpose.

🧠 I’m constantly learning and pushing myself to improve.  
Whether it’s exploring new technologies, improving problem-solving skills, or understanding how systems work at a deeper level, I like staying curious and evolving with every project I take on.

🤝 I enjoy working with people just as much as I enjoy working with code.  
Collaboration, communication, and sharing ideas are a big part of how I grow. I believe the best results come from strong teamwork, different perspectives, and a willingness to learn from others.

⚽ Sports play a big role in my life—they keep me sharp, competitive, and disciplined.  
They’ve taught me consistency, resilience, and how to perform under pressure, and I bring that same mindset into my work and projects every day.

🚀 I’m driven by progress and motivated by challenges.  
I like stepping outside my comfort zone, building things that matter, and improving one step at a time—both as a developer and as a person.

🔥 Always pushing forward. Always building. One project at a time.
